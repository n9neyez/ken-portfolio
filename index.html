A little about me

Expressing oneself through technology... poetic

My whole life I've been around computers and always had a passion for art, whether it’s visual art, music, fashion, digital or whatever media one chooses to express themselves through; I appreciate creative expression. Technology is constantly evolving, shaping our very future and I want to be a part of the evolution that is already taking place. It's a lot like art in a way. There are types and avenues you can explore, but you might still call those different explorations “Technology.”

Humility and empathy go a long way in improving the present and developing the future. As we advance in technology, it’s important to develop solutions in how we experience these technologies. With User Experience Design, I can help shape these experiences.
